About the Workshop

A joint workshop bringing together researchers from Korea and Japan

This workshop aims to foster collaboration and exchange of ideas among researchers working on the mathematical analysis of chemotaxis models. The workshop will feature talks on recent developments in the theory of partial differential equations arising in chemotaxis, including questions of global existence, blow-up phenomena, boundedness, and asymptotic behavior of solutions. Researchers from leading institutions in Korea and Japan will present their latest results and discuss open problems in the field.
